Strawberry Salsa Recipe

A sweet salsa that is great with pork and chicken dishes.

Ingredients

  • 1 pint fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 4 roma (plum) tomatoes, seeded and chopped
  • 1 jalapeno peppers, seeded and minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lime, juiced

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine strawberries, tomatoes, chile peppers, garlic, lime juice and oil.
  2. Toss all together to mix and coat.
  3. Cover dish and refrigerate for 2 hours to chill. Ready to serve!
